# Ramen Noodles

## Field Use Summary

Ramen noodles are one of the strongest base ingredients for fast MiniMo meals.

## Common Field Form

The most common field form is instant ramen noodle packets.

## Best Uses

- trail lunches
- emergency meals
- ramen upgrade bowls
- cold-weather brothy meals

## Common Pairings

- beef jerky
- chicken packets
- tuna packets
- peanut butter
- dehydrated vegetables
- hot sauce
- soy sauce packets

## MiniMo Notes

Ramen can foam quickly in the MiniMo. Reduce heat after the noodles begin to soften.
