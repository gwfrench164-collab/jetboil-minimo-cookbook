# Chicken Packets

## Summary

Shelf-stable protein for rice, pasta, wraps, soups, and skillet meals.

## Why Carry It

Chicken Packets is useful because it is shelf-stable, practical for camp cooking, and connects to multiple recipes or meal systems.

## Common Forms

- pouch chicken
- foil chicken packet
- canned chicken alternative

## Field Uses

- Quick camp meals
- MiniMo-compatible cooking
- Pantry module building
- Recipe substitutions

## Related Recipes

- Camp Chicken Fried Rice
- Angel Hair Pasta with Chicken and Artichokes
- Sesame Chicken

## Related Techniques

- Backpacking Protein Systems
- Trail Pantry Essentials
- Just Add Water Meals

## Substitutions

- freeze-dried chicken
- tuna packets
- salmon packets

## Storage Notes

Store sealed and protected from moisture. Pack in a secondary bag if leaks, punctures, or odor are likely.

## MiniMo Notes

Drain if needed; add late to avoid overcooking.
