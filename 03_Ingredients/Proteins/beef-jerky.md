# Beef Jerky

## Summary

No-cook protein that can also flavor brothy noodle meals.

## Why Carry It

Beef Jerky is useful because it is shelf-stable, practical for camp cooking, and connects to multiple recipes or meal systems.

## Common Forms

- jerky
- dried beef
- beef stick

## Field Uses

- Quick camp meals
- MiniMo-compatible cooking
- Pantry module building
- Recipe substitutions

## Substitutions

## Storage Notes

Store sealed and protected from moisture. Pack in a secondary bag if leaks, punctures, or odor are likely.
