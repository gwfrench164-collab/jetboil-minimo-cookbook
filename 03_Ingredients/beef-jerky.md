# Beef Jerky

## Field Use Summary

Beef jerky is a durable shelf-stable protein for ramen, soups, rice meals, and trail lunches.

## Best Uses

- ramen
- brothy soups
- quick protein upgrades
- emergency food

## Field Notes

Cut or tear into smaller pieces for faster softening.
