# Chicken Packets

## Field Use Summary

Chicken packets are a practical shelf-stable substitute for fresh chicken in many MiniMo recipes.

## Best Uses

- ramen
- rice bowls
- pasta
- couscous
- soups

## Field Notes

Add late in cooking to avoid drying out.
