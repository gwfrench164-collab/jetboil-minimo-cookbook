# Dehydrated Vegetables

## Summary

Lightweight vegetable component for soups, ramen, rice, couscous, and pasta.

## Why Carry It

Dehydrated Vegetables is useful because it is shelf-stable, practical for camp cooking, and connects to multiple recipes or meal systems.

## Common Forms

- dried mixed vegetables
- dehydrated peas
- dehydrated carrots
- dried onions

- Quick camp meals
- MiniMo-compatible cooking
- Pantry module building
- Recipe substitutions

## Substitutions

## Storage Notes

Store sealed and protected from moisture. Pack in a secondary bag if leaks, punctures, or odor are likely.
