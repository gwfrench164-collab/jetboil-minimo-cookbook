# Instant Rice

## Summary

Fast carbohydrate base for rice bowls, curries, chili extensions, and fish meals.

## Why Carry It

Instant Rice is useful because it is shelf-stable, practical for camp cooking, and connects to multiple recipes or meal systems.

## Common Forms

- instant white rice
- instant brown rice
- microwave rice pouch

## Best Uses

- backpacking
- car camping
- quick dinners
- bowl meals

## Related Recipes

- Camp Chicken Fried Rice
- Thai Trout
- Keto Chili Recipe

## Related Techniques

- Just Add Water Meals
- Trail Pantry Essentials
- Minimal Ingredient Cooking

## Substitutions

- couscous
- ramen noodles
- small pasta

## Fresh vs Shelf-Stable

- Fresh alternative: cooked rice
- Shelf-stable alternative: instant rice or rice pouch

## Storage Notes

Check package date; dry instant rice keeps well when sealed. Once opened or repackaged, best used within several months if kept dry and sealed.

## MiniMo Notes

Use slightly less water when adding wet ingredients or sauces.

## Warnings or Limitations

- Can become mushy if overhydrated.
- Wet rice pouches are heavier than dry instant rice.
