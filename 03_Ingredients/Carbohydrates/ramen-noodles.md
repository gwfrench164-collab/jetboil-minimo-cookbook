# Ramen Noodles

## Summary

Fast-cooking noodle base for soups, ramen bowls, and trail lunches.

## Why Carry It

Ramen Noodles is useful because it is shelf-stable, practical for camp cooking, and connects to multiple recipes or meal systems.

## Common Forms

- instant ramen block
- ramen noodle packet
- plain ramen noodles

## Field Uses

- Quick camp meals
- MiniMo-compatible cooking
- Pantry module building
- Recipe substitutions

## Related Recipes

- Chi-Chi Ramen Noodles
- Creamy Ramen Chowder

## Related Techniques

- Just Add Water Meals
- Trail Pantry Essentials

## Substitutions

- rice noodles
- angel hair pasta
- instant rice

## Storage Notes

Store sealed and protected from moisture. Pack in a secondary bag if leaks, punctures, or odor are likely.

## MiniMo Notes

Foams quickly; reduce heat after noodles soften.
