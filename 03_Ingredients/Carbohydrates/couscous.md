# Couscous

## Summary

Very fast hydrating base for one-pot meals and cold-weather bowls.

## Why Carry It

Couscous is useful because it is shelf-stable, practical for camp cooking, and connects to multiple recipes or meal systems.

## Common Forms

- instant couscous
- plain couscous

## Field Uses

- Quick camp meals
- MiniMo-compatible cooking
- Pantry module building
- Recipe substitutions

## Related Recipes

## Related Techniques

## Substitutions

- instant rice
- bulgur
- small pasta

## Storage Notes

Store sealed and protected from moisture. Pack in a secondary bag if leaks, punctures, or odor are likely.

## MiniMo Notes

Remove from heat and cover; couscous hydrates quickly.
