# Couscous

## Summary

Very fast hydrating base for one-pot meals and cold-weather bowls.

## Why Carry It

Couscous is useful because it is shelf-stable, practical for camp cooking, and connects to multiple recipes or meal systems.

## Common Forms

- instant couscous
- plain couscous
- seasoned couscous mix

## Best Uses

- backpacking
- cold weather
- fast dinners
- one-pot meals

## Related Recipes

- Chef Dan's Rockin Moroccan Couscous
- Campfire Pizza in a Pot
- Chili Couscous Bowl

## Related Techniques

- Just Add Water Meals
- Trail Pantry Essentials
- Minimal Ingredient Cooking

## Substitutions

- instant rice
- bulgur
- small pasta

## Fresh vs Shelf-Stable

- Fresh alternative: Not especially applicable
- Shelf-stable alternative: instant couscous

## Storage Notes

Check package date; stores well dry. Once opened or repackaged, best used within several months if sealed dry.

## MiniMo Notes

Remove from heat and cover; couscous hydrates quickly without extended simmering.

## Warnings or Limitations

- Hydrates very quickly; too much water makes it pasty.
- Needs fat, sauce, or seasoning for best flavor.
