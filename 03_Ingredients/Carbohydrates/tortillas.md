# Tortillas

## Summary

Durable wrap base for quesadillas, wraps, breakfast meals, and edible plates.

## Why Carry It

Tortillas is useful because it is shelf-stable, practical for camp cooking, and connects to multiple recipes or meal systems.

## Common Forms

- flour tortillas
- small tortillas
- large tortillas

## Field Uses

- Quick camp meals
- MiniMo-compatible cooking
- Pantry module building
- Recipe substitutions

## Substitutions

## Storage Notes

Store sealed and protected from moisture. Pack in a secondary bag if leaks, punctures, or odor are likely.

## MiniMo Notes

Warm gently in skillet; avoid high heat that scorches before fillings warm.
