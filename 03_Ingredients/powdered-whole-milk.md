# Powdered Whole Milk

## Field Use Summary

Powdered whole milk is one of the most useful shelf-stable dairy ingredients for enjoyable camp cooking.

## Best Uses

- chai
- hot chocolate
- oatmeal
- creamy pasta
- breakfast bowls
- sauces

## Field Notes

Add gradually and avoid hard boiling after dairy is added.
