# Bouillon

## Summary

Lightweight broth and flavor base for soups, noodles, rice, and couscous.

## Why Carry It

Bouillon is useful because it is shelf-stable, practical for camp cooking, and connects to multiple recipes or meal systems.

## Common Forms

- bouillon cube
- bouillon powder
- broth concentrate

## Field Uses

- Quick camp meals
- MiniMo-compatible cooking
- Pantry module building
- Recipe substitutions

## Related Recipes

- Backpacker's Pho
- Quick Tortellini Soup
- Beef Jerky Ramen

## Related Techniques

- Just Add Water Meals
- Trail Pantry Essentials
- Minimal Ingredient Cooking

## Substitutions

- broth packet
- stock concentrate
- ramen seasoning

## Storage Notes

Store sealed and protected from moisture. Pack in a secondary bag if leaks, punctures, or odor are likely.

## MiniMo Notes

Concentrated and salty; start small.
