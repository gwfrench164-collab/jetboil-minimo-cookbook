# Powdered Milk

## Summary

Adds creaminess, calories, and dairy flavor to drinks, oats, soups, and sauces.

## Why Carry It

Powdered Milk is useful because it is shelf-stable, practical for camp cooking, and connects to multiple recipes or meal systems.

## Common Forms

- nonfat dry milk
- whole powdered milk
- dry milk

## Best Uses

- backpacking
- car camping
- beverages
- breakfasts
- creamy meals

## Related Recipes

- Mocha Hot Chocolate
- 30 Second Hot Chocolate
- Hot Granola Breakfast

## Related Techniques

- Just Add Water Meals
- Trail Pantry Essentials
- Backcountry Coffee Fundamentals

## Substitutions

- powdered creamer
- powdered coconut milk
- shelf-stable milk

## Fresh vs Shelf-Stable

- Fresh alternative: whole milk
- Shelf-stable alternative: powdered whole milk or nonfat dry milk

## Storage Notes

Check package date; keeps best sealed and dry. Once opened or repackaged, use within package guidance; protect from moisture and odors.

## MiniMo Notes

Mix gradually to avoid clumps; use lower heat for dairy-based mixtures.

## Warnings or Limitations

- Can clump if dumped into hot liquid too quickly.
- Dairy-based mixtures can scorch.
