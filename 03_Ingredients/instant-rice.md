# Instant Rice

## Field Use Summary

Instant rice is a practical grain base for the MiniMo because it cooks quickly and uses less fuel than raw rice.

## Best Uses

- rice bowls
- fried rice
- chicken and rice
- curry rice

## Field Notes

Avoid overhydrating. Stir frequently if cooking with sauce.
