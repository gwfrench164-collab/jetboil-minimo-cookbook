# Residual Heat Finishing

## Purpose

Use retained heat in Jetboil cookware to finish sensitive foods without direct flame.

## Core Method

1. Bring food nearly to finished doneness.
2. Turn burner off early.
3. Leave lid on when appropriate.
4. Allow retained heat to finish cooking.
5. Stir occasionally if sauce consistency matters.
