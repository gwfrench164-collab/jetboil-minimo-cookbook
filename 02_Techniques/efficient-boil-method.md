# Efficient Boil Method

## Purpose

Bring water to temperature quickly while minimizing wasted fuel and boil-over events.

## Core Method

1. Use only the amount of water required.
2. Start with lid on whenever practical.
3. Reduce flame once full boil is reached.
4. Avoid prolonged rolling boils.
5. Add ingredients promptly once water reaches temperature.
