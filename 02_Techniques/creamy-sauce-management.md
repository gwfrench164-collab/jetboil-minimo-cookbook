# Creamy Sauce Management

## Purpose

Prevent dairy- and starch-based sauces from separating, scorching, or becoming grainy in compact high-efficiency cookware.

## Core Method

1. Reduce heat before adding dairy.
2. Stir continuously once cheese or cream is added.
3. Avoid returning creamy sauces to a hard boil.
4. Add powdered milk slowly to prevent clumping.
5. Remove briefly from heat if thickening too aggressively.
