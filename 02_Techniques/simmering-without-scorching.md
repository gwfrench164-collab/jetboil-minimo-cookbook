# Simmering Without Scorching

## Purpose

Prevent sauces, dairy, starches, and sugars from burning on the MiniMo pot.

## Core Method

1. Bring ingredients to a boil only briefly.
2. Reduce heat aggressively once simmering begins.
3. Stir more often than would normally be required on a home stove.
4. Rotate the pot slightly if needed to distribute heat.
5. Remove briefly from heat if scorching begins.

## MiniMo-Specific Notes

The MiniMo concentrates heat efficiently near the flux-ring base. Recipes containing cheese, sugar, milk, cream, or starch require lower flame settings than many users expect.

## Common Mistakes

- Using excessive flame during simmering
- Leaving sugary sauces unstirred
- Boiling dairy-based sauces
- Filling pot too close to the top during simmer recipes
