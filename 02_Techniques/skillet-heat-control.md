# Skillet Heat Control

## Purpose

Prevent rapid overheating and uneven browning in the JetBoil skillet.

## Core Method

1. Preheat gradually instead of using maximum flame.
2. Keep burner output lower than expected.
3. Move skillet frequently during cooking.
4. Stir or flip foods earlier than on home cookware.
5. Reduce flame before adding sugars, sauces, or tortillas.

## Common Mistakes

- Preheating on high
- Leaving tortillas stationary too long
- Using excessive oil
- Adding cheese before reducing heat
