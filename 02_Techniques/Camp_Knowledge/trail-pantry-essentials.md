# Trail Pantry Essentials

A strong trail pantry lets many meals share the same ingredients.

## Core Pantry Categories

- Carbohydrate bases
- Protein sources
- Fat sources
- Flavor systems
- Drink systems
- Snack systems

## High-Value Items

- Tortillas
- Ramen noodles
- Instant rice
- Couscous
- Oatmeal
- Powdered milk
- Olive oil
- Bouillon
- Dehydrated vegetables
- Chicken, tuna, and salmon packets
- Jerky
- Nut butter packets

## Field Note

The best pantry ingredients are useful in more than one recipe.
