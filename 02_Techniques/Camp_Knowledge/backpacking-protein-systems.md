# Backpacking Protein Systems

Protein is one of the most important parts of a satisfying trail meal.

## Best Lightweight Protein Forms

- Chicken packets
- Tuna packets
- Salmon packets
- Beef jerky
- Dried meats
- Cheese
- Protein bars
- Nut butter packets

## Packets vs Cans

Packets are better for backpacking because they are lighter, easier to pack, and do not require a can opener.

## Meal Pairings

- Tuna + tortillas
- Chicken + rice
- Jerky + ramen
- Cheese + tortillas
- Peanut butter + oatmeal

## Field Note

Protein should be considered both as a meal component and as a snack system.
