# Coffee Brewing Methods

Different camp coffee methods make different tradeoffs.

## Instant Coffee

Fastest and lightest. Best when convenience matters more than flavor.

## Cowboy Coffee

Boil water, remove from heat, add grounds, stir, and let grounds settle. Use about 1 tablespoon coffee per 6 ounces water. Minimal gear, but gritty.

## Percolator

Good for group camp coffee, but overheating can make the coffee bitter.

## AeroPress

Compact and durable. Uses about 2.5 tablespoons ground coffee for one cup. Best for single servings.

## Pour Over

High flavor quality, but takes time and attention. Better for slower mornings than quick trail departures.

## French Press

Simple and rich. For a 10 oz cup, use about 3 tablespoons coffee for lighter brew or 5 tablespoons for strong brew. Steep about 4 minutes.
