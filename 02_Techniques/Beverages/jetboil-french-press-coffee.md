# JetBoil French Press Coffee

The JetBoil French press workflow is one of the best coffee methods for your setup because you already have the MiniMo and Grande Coffee Press.

## Ratio

For a 10 oz cup:

- 3 tablespoons coffee = lighter brew
- 5 tablespoons coffee = stronger brew

## Method

1. Boil water.
2. Let the water stop actively boiling.
3. Add coarse-ground coffee.
4. Stir.
5. Steep about 4 minutes.
6. Press slowly.
7. Pour and serve.

## Compatibility Note

The Grande Coffee Press fits the MiniMo, SUMO, and Stash systems.
