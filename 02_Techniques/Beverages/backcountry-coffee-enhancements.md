# Backcountry Coffee Enhancements

## Hot Cocoa Mix
Add hot cocoa mix to coffee to taste.

## Powdered Milk + Instant Coffee
Use powdered milk to reduce bitterness and create a creamier drink.

## Caramel Coffee
Place two soft caramels in a mug and pour hot coffee over them.

## Premade Camp Coffee
Prepare coffee before the trip and carry it in a thermos for car camping.

## Camp Coffee System
Use a JetBoil coffee press with fresh grounds for brewed coffee.
