# Backcountry Coffee Fundamentals

Good backcountry coffee depends more on matching the method to the trip than following one rigid rule.

## Core Principles

- Match the grind to the brew method.
- Grind shortly before brewing when practical.
- Start around a 1:16 coffee-to-water ratio.
- Adjust strength to taste.

## French Press Starting Point

- Coarse grind
- 750 ml water
- About 50 g coffee, or roughly 7 tablespoons
- Steep at least 4 minutes

## Pour Over Starting Point

- Medium-fine grind
- 350 ml water
- About 26 g coffee, or roughly 3.5 tablespoons
- Let the coffee bloom 45–60 seconds between first and second pour

## Field Note

Coffee is not only caffeine. It is also a morale ritual. A good coffee method can make camp mornings much better.
