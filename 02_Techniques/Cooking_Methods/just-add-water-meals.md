# Just Add Water Meals

Just-add-water meals are built to reduce cooking and cleanup in camp. The core meal framework is:

```text
Carbohydrate base
+ fat or oil
+ vegetables
+ protein
+ spices
```

## Best Bases

- Instant rice
- Small pasta
- Rice noodles
- Couscous
- Instant oatmeal

## Fats and Flavor

Powdered milk, powdered butter, olive oil, and bouillon can add calories and richness. Oils and pastes should usually be packed separately.

## Vegetables

Dehydrated vegetables, dried onions, dried mushrooms, and dried fruit all work well.

## Proteins

Freeze-dried chicken, bacon bits, nuts, nut butters, dried beans, and powdered eggs are useful options.

## Camp Workflow

Boil water, remove from heat, add water to the meal, cover, and let sit until hydrated.
