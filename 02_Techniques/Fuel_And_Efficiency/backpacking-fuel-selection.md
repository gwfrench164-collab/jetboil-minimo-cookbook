# Backpacking Fuel Selection

Fuel selection depends on the stove, trip conditions, and how much cooking or boiling you plan to do.

## Best Default for MiniMo Use

For most backpacking use, a propane/isobutane canister blend is the practical default because it balances:

- pack weight
- performance
- fuel availability
- simplicity
- broad temperature usability

## When Other Fuels Make Sense

- Propane: better for car camping and basecamp systems.
- White gas: useful in extreme cold, but more demanding.
- Alcohol: light and simple, but slower.
- Wood: dependent on fire rules and dry fuel availability.

## Field Note

Fuel planning becomes more important if you are also boiling water for purification, coffee, tea, or just-add-water meals.
