# Cold Weather Fuel Use

Cold weather changes fuel performance. The main concern is pressure loss in canister fuels.

## Practical Fuel Notes

- Propane performs well in cold temperatures.
- Isobutane is better than standard butane.
- Propane/isobutane blends are the practical default for many backpacking stoves.
- White gas can work in extreme conditions, but is more involved and less beginner-friendly.

## MiniMo Note

For normal cold-weather MiniMo use, compatible propane/isobutane canister fuel is the sensible starting point.
