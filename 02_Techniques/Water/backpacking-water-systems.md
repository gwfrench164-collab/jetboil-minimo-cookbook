# Backpacking Water Systems

A backpacking water system is not just a bottle. It includes:

- collection
- storage
- treatment
- boiling
- cooking
- cleanup

## Core Components

- Durable bottle or bladder
- Filter or purifier
- Backup treatment option
- Stove and fuel for boiling
- Separate handling for untreated water

## MiniMo Connection

The MiniMo can support both cooking and purification, but boiling water for safety uses fuel. Plan accordingly.
