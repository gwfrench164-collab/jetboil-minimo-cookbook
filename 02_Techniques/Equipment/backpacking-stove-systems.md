# Backpacking Stove Systems

Backpacking stove systems should be matched to the food you actually plan to make.

## Fast-Boil Systems

Best for:

- coffee
- tea
- hot chocolate
- instant meals
- dehydrated meals

## Precision-Cook Systems

Best for:

- simmering
- sauces
- skillet cooking
- rice dishes
- meals that can scorch

## Basecamp Systems

Best for:

- group meals
- skillet cooking
- car camping
- larger pots

## MiniMo Note

The MiniMo is valuable because it can boil water but also offers simmer control for real cooking.
