# Backpacking Cookware

Cookware choice depends on how much actual cooking you plan to do.

## Titanium

Best for ultralight boiling and simple meals.

## Anodized Aluminum

Better heat distribution for more complex cooking.

## Skillet Use

A skillet expands the MiniMo ecosystem into:

- quesadillas
- French toast
- egg dishes
- fried rice
- reheating
- wraps

## Field Note

If a meal can scorch, cookware and heat control matter more than raw boil speed.
