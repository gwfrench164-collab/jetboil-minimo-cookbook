# Camp Kitchen Organization

A good camp kitchen system reduces frustration and prevents lost tools.

## Core Items

- Stuff sacks
- Dry bags
- Mesh organizer bags
- Trash bag
- Cleaning kit

## Cleanup

Use biodegradable soap carefully, pack out all food scraps, and avoid attracting wildlife.

## Field Note

Organization matters more as meals become more complex.
