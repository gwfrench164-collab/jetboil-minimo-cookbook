# Outdoor Cooking Planning

Outdoor cooking works best when meals, equipment, water, fuel, and cleanup are planned together.

## Before the Trip

- Choose meals.
- Pack required ingredients.
- Confirm equipment.
- Estimate fuel.
- Practice unfamiliar techniques.

## In Camp

- Organize ingredients before lighting the stove.
- Keep fuel use efficient.
- Clean promptly.
- Pack out trash and food scraps.

## Field Note

Most field cooking problems are planning problems.
