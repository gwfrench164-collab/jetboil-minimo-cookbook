# Ramen Upgrade System

## Purpose

Convert inexpensive ramen into flexible trail meals using modular protein, vegetable, sauce, and spice additions.

## Core Structure

### Base

- ramen noodles
- water
- seasoning packet

### Protein Options

- beef jerky
- chicken packet
- tuna packet
- bacon bits
- freeze-dried meat
- egg

### Vegetable Options

- dehydrated vegetables
- green onions
- spinach
- mushrooms
- peas

### Flavor Systems

- soy sauce
- curry paste
- peanut butter
- hot sauce
- sesame oil
- lime
- coconut milk powder
