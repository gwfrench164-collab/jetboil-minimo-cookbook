# Ramen Pantry Module

## Purpose

A compact, grocery-accessible pantry module for fast MiniMo meals.

## Core Base

- instant ramen
- seasoning packets

## Protein Options

- beef jerky
- chicken packets
- tuna packets
- bacon bits
- freeze-dried meat
- egg, for car camping or short trips

## Vegetable Options

- dehydrated vegetables
- green onions
- spinach
- mushrooms
- peas

## Flavor Additions

- hot sauce
- soy sauce packets
- peanut butter
- sesame oil
- curry powder
- lime packet or True Lime
- coconut milk powder

## Fresh Upgrade

For car camping:

- green onions
- soft-boiled egg
- fresh spinach
- mushrooms
- leftover cooked meat

## Shelf-Stable Version

For backpacking:

- ramen packet
- beef jerky or chicken packet
- dehydrated vegetables
- hot sauce packet
- peanut butter packet or soy sauce packet

## MiniMo Notes

Ramen can foam quickly in the MiniMo. Use the efficient boil method, then reduce heat after noodles are added.

## Recipes Using This Module

- Beef Jerky Ramen
- Chi-Chi Ramen Noodles
