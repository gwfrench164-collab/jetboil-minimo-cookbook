# Keto Chili Recipe

## Ingredients
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 1/2 pound ground beef
- 2 tbsp chili seasoning
- 1 can diced tomatoes
- 8 oz beef broth
- 3 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 tbsp chiles
- 2 tsp chives
- Shredded cheese (to taste)

## Instructions
1. Light stove and add olive oil.
2. Cook onion until translucent.
3. Brown ground beef and add chili seasoning.
4. Move chili to the pot.
5. Add tomatoes, broth, and seasoning.
6. Add tomato paste and chiles; bring to a simmer.
7. Cover and cook 10 minutes or more.
8. Top with chives and cheese.
