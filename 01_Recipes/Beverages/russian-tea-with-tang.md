# Russian Tea with Tang

## Ingredients
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup Tang orange powder
- 1/2 cup instant tea
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp cloves
- 1 package lemonade mix (1 quart size)

## Instructions
1. Mix ingredients and store in zip bag.
2. Mix 2–3 tbsp with 1 cup hot water.
