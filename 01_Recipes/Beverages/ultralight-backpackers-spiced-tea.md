# Ultralight Backpackers' Spiced Tea

## Ingredients
- 2 tubs Crystal Light Peach Tea
- 1 tub Crystal Light Pink Lemonade
- 2 tubs sugar free Tang
- 1/4 tsp ground cloves
- 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon

## Instructions
1. Mix powders thoroughly.
2. Add about 1/2 tsp mix per 8 oz hot water.
3. Adjust to taste.

## Note
Stores well in a small spice bottle with shaker lid.
