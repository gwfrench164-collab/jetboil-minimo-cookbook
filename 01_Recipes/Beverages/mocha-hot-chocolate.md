# Mocha Hot Chocolate

## Ingredients
- 3 1/2 cups dry milk
- 2 3/4 cups powdered chocolate drink mix
- 3/4 cup + 2 tbsp powdered non-dairy creamer
- 1/2 cup + 2 tbsp instant coffee or espresso
- 2 tbsp powdered sugar

## Instructions
1. Mix ingredients together.
2. Store in airtight container.
3. For each serving add 1/4 cup mix to 1 cup boiling water.
4. Stir well.
5. Optional garnish: mini marshmallows, salted caramel, chocolate syrup.

## Note
Original source mentions an optional alcoholic addition. Omitted from this project.
